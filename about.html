A few short years ago, I decided to leave behind the monotony of administrative office work, align my life and career path to my interests, and delved into the world of code. As a little girl growing up in South Africa, the UK and Ireland, I discovered early on that I was driven by a passion for fixing and building things. This lifelong thirst for answers and knowledge led me down many paths but, as a natural problem solver, I was instinctively drawn towards coding, where the creativity of design and the endlessness of technological solutions can make anything happen.
I thrive when challenged and challenge myself to thrive in many environments - whether it's solving riddles, putting together 1000 piece puzzles in an afternoon or applying myself like a mental gymnast to solve a problem using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and PHP. My ambition now is to move towards Full Stack development, a field where I will be able to continue finding solutions and answers while dreaming up beautiful design, which I was inspired to pursue by Build in Amsterdam.
